The Bus Team

Marvel Agents of Shield's focuses on Phil Coulson's hand-picked team facing all sorts of obstacles. This team is often refered to as the bus team because their main form of transportation is a plane called 'The Bus'. This hand-picked group includes all the stereotypes for a secret agent team from genius scientists, skillful field agents, to a hacker asset. Phil Coulson, trained by Director Fury, is the leader of this team, ultimately making all the tough calls on missions. He was previously seen in multiple MCU movies, including The Avengers (2012) where his character is shown dying, though throughout this show they uncover the secrets of his death and how they were able to bring him back to life. Then there is Melinda May, famously known as the Cavalry, who is Coulson's right hand woman. She originally joined the team as just a pilot, but stepped out of her comfort zone to be in the field again. Next, we have Grant Ward, a very serious field agent, who doesn't want to be on the plane as he typically works alone. Then we have the iconic duo, Jemma Simmons and Leopold Fitz, the scientists on the team. Simmons is the biochemist, who is one of the youngest members of SHIELD's research division. Fitz is the engineer of the team, as well as one of the youngest members of SHIELD's Sci-Tech division. And finally we have Skye, who will later be known as Daisy Johnson, the hacker that gets recruited to the team as a consultant, before she soon becomes an official agent after proving her loyalty.

Future Additions

As the seasons go on old characters leave and new characters join the team. The most notable characters are, Agent Antione 'Trip' Triplett, Bobbi Morse, Lance Hunter, Elena 'Yoyo' Rodriguez, Alphonso 'Mack' Mackenzie, and Deke Shaw. Antione Triplett, a lacavy agent and grandsonn to the Howling Commando, joined SHIELD as a specialist with medical training. Bobbi Morse was scouted by Melinda May when she went to the SHIELD Academy of Operations and quickly moved up the ranks of SHIELD as a field agent. Lance Hunter, Morse's ex-husband, is a former British soldier turned mercenary that SHIELD hired after the fall of SHIELD. Elena Rodriguez is a Colombian Inhuman who went through Terrigenesis during the Inhuman Outbreak, gaining th eability to move at super-speed with the condition that she always returned to her starting point. Alphonso Mackenzie is a mechanical engineer and was a former member of SHIELD's engineering division before he eventually takes the role of director in later seasons. And the last addition to the team is Deke Shaw a former scavenger who lived in the Lighthouse in the year 2091 and assisted the team in overthrowing the Kree in his timeline and return them to theirs.

Shield's Main Threats

  • Season 1: Hydra, the terrorist organization bent on world domination with Nazi origins.
  • Season 2: Jiaying and the Inhuman Society, who had a deep hatred for humanity and bid to see the Inhumans rise.
  • Season 3: The Hive, the very first inhuman created by the Kree thousands of years ago and had been banished to the planet Maveth.
  • Season 4: Aida and the LMDs, who were created by Doctor Holden Radcliffe, had the main goal of putting the team into the Framework and replace them with LMDs of themselves.
  • Season 5: The Kree took over and enslaved humans after the destruction of Earth and are determined to kill the team and squashing any hope of their prophecy in the new dystopian future.
  • Season 6: Izel and her alien bat-like creatures have landed on Earth and are looking for the three Monoliths so that she can regain her full power and bring her people to this world.
  • Season 7: The Chronicoms, briefly introduced in Season 5 and 6 are a synthetic alien race that intend to invade Earth an claim it as their own, though when SHIELD stands in their way they timetravel into the past intending on stopping SHIELD before it was even craeted.
